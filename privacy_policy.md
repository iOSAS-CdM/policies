
# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** September 9, 2025

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how a group of 4th Year BSIT students at Colegio de Montalban ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and discloses your information. This policy applies to our entire suite of applications created for our capstone project, including the OSAS API, the iOSAS mobile application, the Administrative Staff desktop application, and the Superadmin desktop application (collectively, the "Services").

This document is intended to inform you, as a user of our Services, about our data practices. As this is a capstone project, our primary goal is academic and functional, but we are committed to protecting your privacy.

## 2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

*   **Personal Identification Information:** Name, email address, student/staff ID number, and other contact details you provide when you register for an account or use our Services.
*   **User-Generated Content:** Information you post or submit through the Services, such as announcements, event details, and comments.
*   **Usage Data:** We may automatically collect information about your device and how you interact with our Services. This may include your IP address, device type, operating system, application usage statistics, and crash data.
*   **Data from Third Parties:** If you use a third-party service (like Google) to log in, we may receive information from that service, such as your name and email address.

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

*   **To Provide and Maintain the Services:** To operate our applications, manage user accounts, and provide core functionality.
*   **To Communicate with You:** To send you important announcements, updates, and support messages.
*   **For Academic and Development Purposes:** To fulfill the requirements of our capstone project, and to analyze and improve the functionality and user experience of our Services.
*   **To Ensure Security:** To monitor for and prevent fraudulent or unauthorized activity.

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal data. We may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

*   **With School Administration:** Information may be accessible to authorized personnel at Colegio de Montalban for administrative purposes.
*   **With Service Providers:** We use third-party services, such as Supabase, to host our database and backend infrastructure. These providers have access to data only to perform these tasks on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.
*   **For Legal Reasons:** We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

## 5. Data Security

We implement reasonable security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

## 6. Data Retention

We will retain your personal information only for as long as is necessary for the purposes set out in this Privacy Policy, primarily for the duration of our capstone project and any subsequent evaluation period.

## 7. Your Rights

Under the Data Privacy Act of 2012 in the Philippines, you have rights including the right to access, correct, and request the deletion of your personal data. To exercise these rights, please contact us.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy within the applications.
